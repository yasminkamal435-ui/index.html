about

The engineer behind the models.

I'm an AI Engineer with a Bachelor's in Computer and Information Science, working across the full lifecycle of AI systems — from data processing pipelines and model training through to API development, containerized deployment, and production monitoring.

My focus sits at the intersection of Large Language Models and applied engineering: building RAG systems and agentic, multi-agent workflows that stay grounded and explainable, not just impressive in a demo.

I also work in Computer Vision with YOLO-based detection, and in Explainable AI — using SHAP and LIME to make model decisions auditable, particularly for high-stakes use cases like credit risk scoring.

I'm currently deepening my skills in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, actively working on projects in both areas — across 10+ projects shipped so far.

Along the way I've built hands-on experience with LLM-based application development, vector databases and semantic search, model evaluation and explainability (SHAP, LIME), and shipping models as real services with FastAPI, Docker, and MLflow — the practical layer that turns a trained model into something people can actually use.
